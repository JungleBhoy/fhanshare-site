Our Story

Built by fans, for fans - with security at the heart

The Problem We're Solving

Every Celtic supporter knows the frustration: you can't make it to a match, but your season ticket seat sits empty while thousands of fans would love to be there. Traditional ticket sharing is risky, complicated, and often breaks club rules.

Meanwhile, touts and scalpers exploit genuine fans, charging extortionate prices and undermining the community spirit that makes Celtic special.

Our Solution

CardShare was born from a simple idea: what if season ticket holders could safely share their seat with trusted contacts, while clubs maintain complete control and security?
