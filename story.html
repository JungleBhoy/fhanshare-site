Our Story

Built by fans, for fans - with security at the heart

The Problem We're Solving

Every Celtic supporter knows the frustration: you can't make it to a match, but your season ticket seat sits empty while thousands of fans would love to be there. Traditional ticket sharing is risky, complicated, and often breaks club rules.

Meanwhile, touts and scalpers exploit genuine fans, charging extortionate prices and undermining the community spirit that makes Celtic special. Empty seats cost clubs atmosphere and revenue, while genuine fans are priced out of supporting their team.
