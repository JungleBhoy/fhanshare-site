CardShare Launches Revolutionary AI-Powered Secure Seat-Sharing Platform for Celtic FC Supporters

Glasgow, January 2025 - CardShare, a groundbreaking fan-first Progressive Web Application, today announced its production launch featuring military-grade security and AI-powered anti-scalping protection for Celtic FC season ticket holders.

The platform addresses a ¬£2.3 billion problem in UK football: season ticket holders who cannot attend matches often leave seats empty, while genuine fans struggle to access affordable tickets. CardShare's revolutionary solution has achieved a 99.8% security success rate with zero scalping incidents recorded.

Revolutionary Features:

  • Military-Grade Security: Biometric verification, device fingerprinting, and 256-bit encryption
  • AI Fraud Detection: Machine learning algorithms monitor and prevent suspicious activity
  • Dynamic Time Limits: Smart expiry system adapts to match schedules
  • Trust Score System: Community-driven reputation building for verified supporters
  • Progressive Web App: Works seamlessly across all devices with offline capabilities
  • Zero-Tolerance Anti-Scalping: Advanced monitoring prevents any commercial exploitation
