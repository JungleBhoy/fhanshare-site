📰 Press Release

CardShare Launches Revolutionary Secure Seat-Sharing Platform for Celtic FC Supporters

Glasgow, January 2025 - CardShare, a groundbreaking fan-first mobile application, today announced its launch to help Celtic FC season ticket holders securely share their matchday seats with trusted contacts through time-limited QR codes.

The platform addresses a long-standing challenge in football: season ticket holders who cannot attend matches often leave seats empty, while genuine fans struggle to access affordable tickets. CardShare's innovative solution prevents scalping while enabling safe, community-driven seat sharing.

Key Features:

  • End-to-End Security: Multi-factor authentication and encrypted QR codes
  • Time-Limited Access: Codes automatically expire 2 hours before kick-off
  • Anti-Scalping Protection: Built-in measures prevent commercial reselling
  • Club Integration: Works seamlessly with existing stadium security systems
  • Fan-First Design: Created by supporters, for supporters
